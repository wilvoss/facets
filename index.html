Guide

In this game you are either creating a word puzzle to send to your friends or guessing the solution to a word puzzle that a friend sent to you.

There is no single player mode. The game requires sharing between friends via text, chat, email, or some other social media.

Creating

When creating a new puzzle, it's your job to come up with clues that connect the four word-pairs that are displayed on the edges of a gem.

Anatomy of a puzzle:

When you are satisfied with your 4 clues, hit the Send button. The puzzle will scramble and you'll be able to share a message with your friend that contains a link to the scrambled puzzle.

They're gonna have to recreate your puzzle based on your clues! 🧠

Guessing

When your friend created this word puzzle, they were given word-pairs that they had to connect with single word clues.

It's your job to figure out what word-pairs they were given based on the clues they came up with.

Anatomy of a solved puzzle:

Click the SEND button to send your guess back to whoever sent it to you.

Click the Guess button to automatically check your guess.

Reviewing

If your friends have "Pass 'n play" enabled in settings, they'll send their guesses back to you. When reviewing, remove incorrectly placed cards from the gem.

Click the SEND button to send the puzzle back, even if you are just giving them the solution.

Controls

A card is a group of four words around the edges of a small outlined square.

You can drag cards from one spot to another spot. While guessing or reviewing, you can rotate the cards by tapping on the curved arrows in the corner of each card.

The gem has 4 large clues around its edges. At any time, you can tap on a clue or either of the two large curved arrows above the gem to rotate it.

Advanced

If you play on multiple browsers, be sure to change your appDataPlayerCurrent ID # in settings to be the same on each so that you can review any game you sent to anyone on any device 🤓.
