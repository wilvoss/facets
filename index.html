Tutorial

Basic gameplay

The challenge of "{{gameName}}" is to connect different words that may or may not be easily connected.

When the gem is a sapphire, you are creating a puzzle. Give 4 clues on the edges of the gem (tap) that you think connects the two words next to the clue. Click the 🧠 button to send the puzzle to a friend (via text, social, etc.).

When the gem is an emerald, you are guessing a puzzle a friend sent you. Drag the cards onto the gem and rotate them so that each large "clue" on the edge of the gem has two small words next to it that make sense. Click the 🤔 button to send your guess back to the puzzle's creator.

When the gem is a ruby, you are reviewing a friend's guess. Remove any cards from the gem that are wrong. Click the 🧠 button to send back, even if you are just giving them the correct solution.

Game controls

A card is a group of four words around the edges of a small square.

You can drag cards from one spot to another spot. While guessing or reviewing, you can rotate the cards by tapping on the curved arrows in the corner of each card.

The gem has 4 large clues around its edges. At any time, you can tap on a clue or either of the two large curved arrows above the gem to rotate it.

Advanced info

If you play on multiple browsers, be sure to change your player number in settings to be the same on each so that you can review any game you sent to anyone on any device 🤓.
